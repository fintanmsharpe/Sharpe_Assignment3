Projects - RoomEase

We interviewed and surveyed hundreds of students, and they all said the same thing:

Why is finding housing to rent for University with my roommates so difficult?

Let us introduce RoomEase. After living in a dorm during our first year at University, we found that schools often leave students to fend for themselves when it comes to finding safe and affordable housing close to campus.

This prototype offers a solution, enabling students to search for housing, sublets, and roommates that match their lifestyle.

Read more about how RoomEase can revolutionize the student-housing search process with our data and early product designing.
