Projects - RoomEase

We interviewed and surveyed hundreds of students, and they all said the same thing:

Why is finding housing to rent for University with my roommates so difficult?

Let us introduce: RoomEase.
After living in a dorm for our first years in University, we found that our schools tend to leave us to fend for ourselves when it comes to finding safe and affordable housing close to the school. This prototype presents a solution that can help students in the search for housing, sublets, and roommates that match their lifestyle.

Upon entering our app, users will be prompted to set up their personal profile, after which they will be able to engage in a simple housing search that doesn’t require the use of multiple housing websites, and gives students the ability the learn about the renting process in order to be more independent and prevent scams.

Read more about how RoomEase can revolutionize the student-housing search process on our slides, which includes a deep dive into our data and early product designing.
