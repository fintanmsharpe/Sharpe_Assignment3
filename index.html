Projects - RoomEase

We interviewed and surveyed hundreds of students, and they all said the same thing:

Why is finding university housing with my roommates so difficult?

Introducing: RoomEase.
After living in a dorm during our first year in University, we found that schools tend to leave students to fend for ourselves when it comes to finding safe and affordable housing. This prototype presents a solution that can help students in the search for housing, sublets, and roommates that match their lifestyle.

Upon entering our app, users will be prompted to set up their personal profile, and then they are able to engage in a simple housing search that doesn’t require the use of multiple housing websites. RoomEase gives students the ability the learn about the renting process in order to be more independent and prevent scams.

Read more about how RoomEase can revolutionize the student-housing search process on our slides, which includes a deep dive into our data and early product designing.
